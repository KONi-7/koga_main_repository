Overnight Soaking Goods

I would like to introduce two goods that can be used for overnight pickup.

coffee

The first is coffee.Isn't it an energy drink?” Some may wonder, “Isn't coffee an energy drink?” Coffee has a stronger stimulant effect than energy drinks and contains more caffeine. It is recommended that you can change the taste to your own preference with milk and sugar.

lemon soda

The second is a ramune.Ramune contains more glucose than other sweets.Glucose, when ingested, supplies nutrients to the brain, which has the effect of activating the brain and relieving fatigue. For this reason, ramune is recommended because it can easily supply energy to a tired brain.
